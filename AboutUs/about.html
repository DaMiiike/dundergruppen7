ABOUT WONDER BOTTLE

It all started with a vision of reducing the every day consume of disposable plastic that unfortunately still is a big issue in the world. Every minute 1 million plastic bottles are sold worldwide and the production of plastic is threatening our planet. The normal yearly waste of one person is around 400kg and the most of that waste is from disposable plastic that is not reusable. Our idea builds on to take action and use our precious access to clean tap water with our Wonder Bottle rather than buying plastic bottles. The production is located in Sweden to minimize the emission of carbon dioxide during shipping and also to strengthen the local production. We behind Wonder Bottle stands for to take up the fight against plastic and do what we can do to create a more sustainable consumption and to inspire more people to make a cleaver choice of bottle.
